9 DAYS TRIP ACROSS LOMBOK AND BALI

Words from the filmer:

We took this trip about a year ago, from August 13 to 22 2014. Personally, this was my very first trip to somewhere outside Java. For that reason, I was so excited about this trip and I'm glad that it did happen and went so well. Although there were a few places that we didn't have the chance to visit, the trip was overall awesome. We met new people, visited cool places and beaches, and just had a really great time. I hope I able to show that in my clips, and I'll be more than happy if you could click the link, amd spend a little bit of time to watch them. Thank you very much to all the people that could make this trip to happen!
